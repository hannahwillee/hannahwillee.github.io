    • A bit about me...

    Hello! My name is Hannah! I study Literature at Edinburgh University and think reading is great! I'm 19 years old and a triplet and grew up in Barcelona and Doha. I literally read all sorts of things from detective stories to poems but mostly I enjoy classic novels.

    Jane Austen once said "The person, be it gentleman or lady, who has not pleasure in a good novel, must be intolerably stupid.Basically what i'm trying to say is that all forms of reading can be insightful, useful and instructive whilst also being enjoyable!

    Here i'm going to review whatever i'm reading at the moment, just because I feel like it!
